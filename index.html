Welcome

We are an organization that promotes effective communication of quantitative analysis. We challenge our speakers to communicate ideas that are meaningful, objective, and simple. People of all education levels are invited to attend or present.

Guidelines

Your presentation must be accessible to a general audience with a high school education. We encourage speakers not to dilute their content but to explain the details that are critical to their idea.

Topic presentations should answer the following questions:

  • What is your idea?
  • What evidence supports your idea?
  • How do we know that the evidence is true?

We also encourage presentations on free tools used in quantitative analysis.

Methodology presentation should answer the following questions:

  • How does the tool/technique add value?
  • When should the tool be used?
  • How do we learn more?

Feedback

At the end of each presentation, the audience is invited to answer the following questions:

  • Do you feel like you understood the main idea of the presentation?
  • Did the presentation effectively communicate the analysis?
  • Did you agree with the conclusion of the presentation?
